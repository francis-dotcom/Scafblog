# Cursor AI: The Only Coding Assistant That Actually Gets It

Then there's Cursor.

## It Kills the Pain Points

Remember when you'd type a comment like "create user auth middleware" and your AI would suggest... a console.log()? Cursor doesn't do that. It actually reads your entire codebase before making suggestions.
