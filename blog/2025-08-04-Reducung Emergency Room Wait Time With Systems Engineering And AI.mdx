## 🔍 Why This Project Matters

Unlike academic research, this project was rooted in **real operational constraints** and **stakeholder realities**.

- Staff burnout and budget limits were real.
- AI models had to be explainable to hospital directors.
- Solutions had to work — not just simulate well.

By bringing together engineering, analytics, and empathy, this project delivered **a measurable reduction in wait times**, without requiring a full systems overhaul.
